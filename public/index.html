Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a monthly subscription?

Absolutely not. SagaForge AI is explicitly a product-based business. You pay a one-time fee for each adventure module you choose to generate and download. There are no recurring fees.

What do I actually get when I make a purchase?

You receive a high-resolution, watermark-free PDF file of the complete adventure module. This is a tangible digital asset that you download and own forever. You can print it, use it on a tablet, or share it with your players.

What TTRPG systems are supported?

We are launching with support for Dungeons & Dragons 5th Edition (using the SRD), Pathfinder 2e, and a "System Agnostic" option. We plan to add more systems based on user demand.

Can I edit the generated adventure?

The output is a final PDF product, which is not designed to be edited like a text document. However, as the Game Master, you are always free to adapt, modify, and be inspired by the content at your table!
